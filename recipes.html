Chinese Dumplings

Ingredients:

NameQuantity
All Purpose Flour2 cups
Warm water1 cup
Ground Pork1/2 lb
Garlic Cloves2
Soy Sauce1 tablespoon
Green Onions2 Finely Chopped

Method:

  1. Make the Dumpling Dough::

    2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Gradually add warm water while stirring with a fork or chopsticks.

    Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

    Cover with a damp towel and let it rest for 30 minutes.

  2. Prepare the Filling:

    3. In a mixing bowl, combine ground meat, chopped napa cabbage, green onions, ginger, garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, sugar, white pepper, and salt.

    Mix well until the filling is evenly combined.

  3. Roll Out the Wrappers:

    4. Divide the dough into small pieces (about the size of a ping-pong ball).

    Roll each piece into a thin circle, about 3 inches in diameter.

  4. Assemble the Dumplings:

    5. Place a small spoonful of filling in the center of a wrapper. Moisten the edges of the wrapper with a little water.

    Fold the wrapper in half and pinch the edges together to seal, pleating if desired.

  5. Cook the Dumplings:
    • Boiled Dumplings:

      • Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add dumplings in batches and cook for 4‚Äì6 minutes until they float to the top.

    • Pan-Fried Dumplings:

    Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Arrange dumplings in the skillet and cook until the bottoms are golden brown. Add 1/4 cup of water, cover, and steam for 5 minutes. Remove the lid and cook until the water evaporates.

  6. Serve:

Mix the dipping sauce ingredients in a small bowl. Serve dumplings hot with the sauce on the side.
